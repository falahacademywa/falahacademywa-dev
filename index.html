Our Curriculum Partners

We partner with trusted organizations to deliver a world-class Islamic and academic education.

MESBA — Islamic Curriculum

Maktab Education Services Board of America. Providing structured Islamic studies, Quran education, and teacher development for Islamic schools across North America.

Visit mesba.org

Red Comet — Academic Curriculum

Accredited K–12 academic curriculum, including the Washington State ALE Program. Pioneering digital education since 1999 with 115+ core courses.

Visit redcomet.org

iCode Renton — STEM & Technology

Coding and technology education for young learners at the Renton, WA location, preparing students for the digital world from an early age.

Visit icodeschool.com

Why Falah Academy?

Families choose Falah Academy because they want more than a school — they want a place where their child's faith is nurtured alongside their education.

  • Kent's first and only full-time Islamic school
  • Dual curriculum — Islamic studies and accredited academics
  • Classes held within Al Huda Islamic Center
  • Certified partners: MESBA, Red Comet & iCode
  • Small class sizes with dedicated, caring teachers
  • A welcoming environment for the entire family

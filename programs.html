Curriculum Partners

MESBA — Islamic Curriculum

Maktab Education Services Board of America (MESBA) provides our Islamic curriculum. MESBA strengthens Islamic education across North America through curriculum support, teacher training, and student assessments for Islamic schools and Hifz programs.

At Falah Academy, MESBA's curriculum covers Quran recitation and memorization, Islamic studies, Arabic language fundamentals, and character development grounded in the Sunnah.

Visit mesba.org

Red Comet — Academic Curriculum

Red Comet is an accredited online school and pioneer in digital education since 1999. They provide Falah Academy with a comprehensive K–12 academic curriculum covering all core subjects including Language Arts, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies.

Red Comet is approved under the Washington State Alternative Learning Experience (ALE) Program, ensuring our students receive a fully accredited academic education.

Visit redcomet.org

iCode Renton — STEM & Technology

iCode School brings coding and technology education to Falah Academy students through their Renton, WA location. Their programs introduce young learners to computational thinking, coding fundamentals, and digital creativity from an early age.

In today's digital world, technology literacy is as essential as reading and writing. iCode ensures our students are prepared for the future they will inherit.

Visit icodeschool.com
